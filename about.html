About Me

I have been interested in technology since an early age. I built my first computer when I was 13 and have been working with them ever since. I started in IT as remote help desk and have worked my way up to deploying whole domains and networks. My greatest skill is my ability to learn. This gift has helped me evolve my IT skills into a career. I am a driven and motivated individual, who strives to be the best at what I do. I have solid work ethic and love working in an environment where I can use the skills I have, while developing more. I really enjoy challenges, and solving complex problems. I've done everything from setting up businesses from the start to troubleshooting server and network issues and more. I've managed people and technology, and love being able to interact with both. I'm always up for a good challenge or hard problem.

In my current role as Cybersecurity Engineer with Document Resources Ltd, I work as part of a large cybersecurity team to maintain cybersecurity best practices, ensure sensitive data and documents are secure, and anticipate possible threats to keep the organization a step ahead. Over the course of my career with Document Resources, I have reduced security risks by more than 33% by implementing physical access controls and a multi-layer security system that includes antispyware and antivirus software.
